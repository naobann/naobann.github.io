Conclusions and Top Achievers

This study attempts to build a predicting and decision typology model based on statistical(regression, cluster analysis) analysis of historic TOD data to provide clear guidelines for site selection and choice of development strategy given specific goals for decision-makers in public transit agency. The dataset is 145 station-areas with TOD interventions between 1995 and 2005 in 15 states. Click to see each station.

Promoting economic development and increasing transit ridership are the two most frequently identified goals by transit agencies (Cervero 2004). Two performance indexes, Economic Development Index (EDI) and Travel Pattern Change Index (TPCI) are used to evaluate the success of TOD under goals of economic development and increase transit Ridership respectively, both of which are historic percent change index from 2005 to 2015. EDI consists of Unemployment rate decrease, Percent property value increase, Vacancy rate decrease, Median household income increase, and Percent population density increase.
